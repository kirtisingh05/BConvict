About Us

We are passionate about using technology to make the world a safer place and believe that our blockchain-based criminal record system is a crucial step towards achieving this goal.

Our Team

Our mission is to provide a secure, decentralized platform that allows law enforcement agencies, employers, and other authorized personnel to access criminal records quickly and easily, while ensuring the privacy and security of the individuals involved.
